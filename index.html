What makes us happy?




The World Happiness Report has investigated happiness across the globe annually since 2012. It ranks the countries from 1 (the happiest country) to 156 (the unhappiest country), and for the last two years Finland has been in the very top, followed by its neighbors Denmark and Norway. Sweden hits the list on 7th place.

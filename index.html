What makes people happy?




The World Happiness Report has investigated happiness across the globe annually since 2012. It ranks the countries from 1 (the happiest country) to 156 (the unhappiest country), and for the last two years Finland has been in the very top, followed by its neighbors Denmark and Norway. Sweden hits the list on 7th place.

Can money buy happiness?

Turns out it can - to a certain extent

Do not work to much though!

Working to much can make you unhappy.

Earning more money can increase your happiness up to a point. After a while the happiness increase flattens out and can even decrease. People who work less also seem to generally be happier. So a healthy work to life balance seems important.
